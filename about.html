About Our Company

A Friendly Candle

Drop candles is an eco-friendly family owned company focused in the upcycling of empty glass bottles of all kinds into 100% all natural candles. The wax we use is a renewable resource and has a smoother burn that last twice as long as normal paraffin wax. Paraffin wax is actually a byproduct of crude oil.

The FDA still allows the usage of paraffin wax in all commercially sold candles but soy wax is the healthier alternative.

Our Mission

Our mission to our customers is to uphold the utmost honesty and conscientous thought about our community by reducing the contribution of empty glass bottles to landfills. Drop candles services multiple local wineries and breweries.

We hope that you our satisfied with the quality craftminship of our candles as much as we enjoy making them in all their different sizes!
